What The Seam Does

The Seam is a live intelligence engine that searches current WFM and ECP industry literature, then synthesizes findings into structured, practitioner-ready summaries.

  • Searches current research papers and vendor publications
  • Synthesizes findings from multiple sources
  • Surfaces actionable insights and best practices
  • Returns structured results with source attribution
  • Falls back to deep training knowledge when live search is unavailable

Best Use Cases

The Seam is built for practitioners who need current, structured intelligence — not a general-purpose chatbot.

  • Researching new WFM or AI agent platforms before evaluation
  • Staying current on AI automation trends in workforce ops
  • Building business cases for WFM investment
  • Preparing for vendor conversations with current context
  • Understanding academic and consulting research on WFM topics
  • Benchmarking against industry best practices
